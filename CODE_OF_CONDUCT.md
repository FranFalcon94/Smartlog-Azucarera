# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insulting or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact the maintainers privately.
